KFound is a charity initiative

Kina Inspires Foundation is a charitable foundation with the purpose of putting smiles on the faces of the street children, orpahans, aged people, physically challenged, less privileged and as well as those accomplishing the dream of education, accessibility in the lives of the children and people in deprived areas.

We are an NGO that is unwaveringly geared towards improving quality of lives for others from community level, national up to global level seeing to their environmental and social needs including their human rights
