About Us

Who We Are.

The foundation is established in Ghana by the determined Philanthropist Sakinatu Ibrahim Abban in January 2020, with two other directors Sadik Twum Ampofo and Issah Ibrahim Abban, Sule Malik the secretary. The foundation was later registered under NGO registration act 179 of 1963. We have our astute members and volunteers in all the 16 regions. We trade on hopes and prayers to open branches all over the country and beyond. Kina inspires foundation is resolute on putting smiles on faces of the street children, orphans, aged people, physically challenged, less privileged in the society

Activities


HEALTH SCREENING:

We undertake health screening in deprived areas where there are high records of fever and other diseases. Our Doctors and Nurses carry out screening exercises to improve the health of the individuals in such communities.

EDUCATIONAL TOUR:

The Kina inspires foundation visits deprived communities and support children with educational materials and also educate, support those who are yet not in school to get access to education.

REACHING OUT / VISITATION TO ORPHANAGE HOMES, PRISONS, HOSPITALS AND OTHERS

The foundation visit orphanages, prisons, hospitals, schools and the likes to extend our support to them, this reaching out is in many forms like giving money to them, food, clothes, anything to support the less privileged homes to impact positively

HEIGHT BUILDING PROJECT:o HEIGHT BUILDING PROJECT:

The foundation help to train, develop and strengthen the skills people need to survive. It also helps people to improve and retain skills, knowledge, tools and other resources needed to do their jobs competently to a greater height. Most people in deprived communities are supported by the Kina inspires foundation to get jobs in order to earn a living.

Vision

A world in which every individual gets access to good living, exterminating poverty and embracing a peaceful space in the world and knowing our human rights.

In our envisioned future, we aspire to create a world where every individual has unfettered access to a quality standard of living, our vision extends beyond mere existence; we envision a global community that eradicates poverty, fostering a harmonious and peaceful coexistence among its inhabitants.

Mission

To provide for the underprivileged through research, advocacy, reaching out and education (RARE) means. Our mission is grounded in a commitment to uplift the underprivileged segments of our society through a multifaceted approach. Utilizing research, advocacy, reaching out (outreach) and education as our cornerstones, we aim to address to address the root causes of poverty. Through meticulously conducted research, we seek to understand the challenges faced by the underprivileged, allowing us to develop target and effective solutions.

Advocacy forms a pivotal part of our mission, as we strive to be the voice of the voiceless. By leveraging our platform, we aim to influence policies that promote inclusivity and socioeconomic equality. Our outreach initiates are designed to directly impact communities in need, providing them with essential resources and support.

Education is a rare and transformative tool in our arsenal. By investing in education, we empower individuals to break free from the cycle of poverty, equipping them with the essential resources and support.

Aims and Focus

To advocate the need for good living, exterminating poverty, good health and embracing a peaceful space in the world.
